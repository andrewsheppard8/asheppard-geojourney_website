Welcome! I’m Andrew Sheppard. This is my GeoJourney — a place where my travels, maps, and stories come together. I created this site to practice working with databases while preparing for a big trip abroad, and it has grown into a platform for sharing adventures and geographic insights.

As a GIS developer, I wanted to explore different libraries and techniques for sharing geographic data. I built this site entirely from scratch, using only free tools and libraries. It’s the culmination of that passion, combining interactive maps, galleries, and blog stories from my travels.

Explore interactive maps, browse photos, and read stories about the people, places, and experiences behind each journey. Whether you’re a fellow explorer or just curious about the world, there’s something here for you.
